About

Two disciplines,
one vision

Michael Gruber is an architect and painter based in Lexington, Massachusetts. His work explores the boundary between the built environment and artistic expression — bringing the same sensitivity to form, light, and material whether designing a space or composing a canvas.

Rooted in the rich architectural and cultural landscape of New England, Michael's practice is defined by an attention to detail, a respect for context, and a belief that beauty and function are never truly separate.
